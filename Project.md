# Heart Guardian

An AI-powered cardiac monitoring platform designed to support early arrhythmia detection, continuous heart health monitoring, and emergency response for patients, doctors, and caregivers.

---

## About

Heart Guardian is a digital healthcare platform that empowers patients living with arrhythmia by providing continuous heart rate monitoring, AI-driven health insights, and emergency response coordination.

The platform connects three user roles:

- Patient
- Doctor
- Caregiver

to create a collaborative healthcare ecosystem.

---

## Problem Statement

Cardiovascular diseases remain one of the leading causes of death worldwide. Many arrhythmia patients experience delayed treatment due to the lack of continuous monitoring and early warning systems.

Heart Guardian aims to provide intelligent monitoring and personalized recommendations while facilitating rapid communication between patients, caregivers, and healthcare professionals.

---

## Solution

Heart Guardian combines Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and modern healthcare technologies to provide:

- Continuous heart rate monitoring
- AI-powered health recommendations
- Risk level prediction
- Emergency notification workflow
- Doctor monitoring dashboard
- Caregiver monitoring dashboard
